About Johnny

Johnny had the great privilege of working with some of the top developers in the video game industry, including none other than former Acclaim President, David Perry. At the time, Johnny didn't know what he was working on, but knew it had to be something important after he signed many disclosures. Mr. Perry's company, Gaikai, was eventually sold to Sony Computer Entertainment and was rebranded as, "PlayStation Now." Johnny had the pleasure of also working with NoFear, Ford, and the Knights of Columbus, all on a national scale. He is now working on Gamerdium, an epitome of what GameStop should be, with semi-pro gamer, Jonathan Amaral. Johnny is currently attending UNH to pursue his certification. He looks forward to class every day.
